# Pattern Stretching

## Place Along

The pattern is placed along the shape as is, without any deformations. It will be rotated to match the angle of the shape, but it will not be stretched. The spacing between pattern repetitions will increase the gaps between pattern instances.

## Stretch To Fit

The pattern is stretched to fit the shape as tightly as possible. Spacing will not leave gaps between pattern instances.

# Pattern Alignment

## Center

The pattern is centered on the shape.

## Outside

The pattern is aligned to the outside of the shape. The center of the pattern will be placed at a distance of half of the pattern size from the shape.

## Inside

The pattern is aligned to the inside of the shape. The center of the pattern will be placed at a distance of half of the pattern size from the shape.
